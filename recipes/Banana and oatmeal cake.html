Banana and oatmeal cake recipe

Description

In this preparation you will learn how to make a moist, healthy banana and oatmeal cake, suitable for vegans and for those who are not experts in the kitchen. It is a preparation high in fiber and low in fat that you can prepare to enjoy with an infusion such as coffee or tea for breakfast or a snack. In addition, we will tell you how to modify it according to your taste and habits, don't miss all the tips we have to share with you.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 dessert spoons of chia seeds
  • 6 dessert spoons of water
  • ½ cup of oil
  • ½ cup of sugar (100 grams)
  • 1 cup of flour 0000
  • ½ cup oats
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ¼ cucharada postre de bicarbonato de sodio
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. The first step to make the banana and oatmeal cake or cake is to hydrate the chia seeds in water. To do this, take them to a container and add 3 times their volume. That is, for 2 teaspoons of seeds, we will add 6 of water. This will cause them to release a gel that will perfectly replace the structure that the egg provides to the cake. If you are not vegan and want to use egg, then you will need to process it by continuing to step 3.
  2. When the seeds have rested at room temperature for about 20 minutes and are gelatinous, put them in a blender along with the oil.
  3. Also add the banana and sugar. If the banana is not ripe, you can put it in the oven with its peel until it turns black. In this way, we will make it add more sweet flavor to the preparation. If you use egg, then process it at this point along with the oil, banana and sugar
  4. Cuando tengas una preparación homogénea y lisa, llévala a un recipiente para continuar agregando el resto de los ingredientes
  5. Agrega la mitad de la harina, previamente tamizada, junto con el polvo para hornear y el bicarbonato de sodio. Si lo deseas, puedes reemplazar la harina por integral o de avena, el resultado será igual de delicioso, pero aportará más fibra. También puedes usar harina leudante. En ese caso, no será necesario que agregues el polvo para hornear. Integra los ingredientes.
  6. Agrega el resto de la harina y la avena. Integra nuevamente con movimientos envolventes y evalúa la consistencia de la preparación de tu bizcocho de avena y plátano esponjoso.
  7. Vuelca la preparación de tu bizcocho en el molde donde desees cocinarlo. En este caso, es uno de 24 x 9 x 6 cm, que previamente hemos engrasado y enharinado, para facilitar el desmolde del bizcocho. Cocínalo en el horno precalentado a 180 ºC por unos 35 minutos o hasta que, al pincharlo, no salgan restos de la preparación en el palillo o cuchillo.
  8. Reserva hasta que enfríe para desmoldarlo. ¡A comer! Cuéntanos si lo pruebas y comparte la fotografía del resultado final.
