Pizza hawaiana

descripcion

Hawaiian pizza is a pizza that contains a base of pomodoro sauce, melted mozzarella cheese and ham and pineapple as toppings.

ingredientes

  • 1 pizza dough
  • Fried tomato
  • 4 pineapple slices
  • Grated cheese
  • Oregano (optional)
  • 4 or 5 slices of cooked ham

Steps

  1. The first thing we will do is prepare the ingredients.
  2. To do this, we will cut the ham into pieces and cut the pineapple into cubes.
  3. Once this is done, we spread the dough for this Hawaiian pizza well, so that it has a uniform thickness. Once this is done, we spread the dough with fried tomato and then cover with the grated cheese.
  4. Now, we will distribute the cooked ham and pineapple on top.
  5. Whoever wants, at this moment, can sprinkle a little oregano. We place our pizza in the oven preheated to 240ÂºC, for approximately 12 minutes, or until the dough begins to turn golden brown.
  6. Once the baking time has elapsed, we take the Hawaiian pizza out of the oven, and we can now serve it and enjoy it.
